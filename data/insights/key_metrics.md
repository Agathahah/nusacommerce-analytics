## Key Insights for Presentation

1. **Regional Focus:** Java Island (Jawa Barat, Jawa Tengah, Jawa Timur, DKI Jakarta) contributes ~62% of total revenue

2. **Customer Retention:** Champions segment (86 customers) drives disproportionate revenue - focus retention efforts here

3. **Payment Shift:** Digital payments (61.98%) overtaking COD - optimize checkout for digital-first

4. **Seasonality:** August peak suggests back-to-school or promotional period - plan inventory accordingly

5. **Cancellation Hotspots:** Higher cancellation rates in non-Java regions - investigate logistics issues
